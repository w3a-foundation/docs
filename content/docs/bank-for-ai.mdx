# The Bank for AI

The Alliance is not only the bank for human-scale customers. It is **the
bank for AI**: the substrate that serves agentic AI workloads as a
first-class customer category, with infrastructure built to the shape of
AI economics rather than retrofitted from human-scale financial-services
tooling.

## Why AI is a categorically new customer

Agentic AI changes the shape of every assumption a traditional
financial-services franchise makes about its customer:

| Dimension | Human-scale customer | Agentic AI customer |
|---|---|---|
| **Transaction frequency** | Hundreds per year | Hundreds per minute (2–4 orders of magnitude higher) |
| **Transaction size** | $10K-class discretionary | Sub-cent micropayments (2–4 orders of magnitude lower) |
| **Identity model** | Government-ID-anchored KYC | Programmatic cryptographic identity, verifiable credentials |
| **Risk posture** | Discretionary risk-officer review | Deterministic spend-cap enforcement |
| **Settlement preference** | T+1 / T+2 acceptable | Deterministic-and-instant required |
| **Auditability** | Discretionary, after-the-fact | Full-trace-to-operator, private-from-competitors |

No incumbent financial-services franchise serves this customer well. The
incumbent rails are KYC-anchored, T+1 / T+2 settled, batched, and priced
for the $10K discretionary-trade shape. **Charging an agent $0.30 for a
transaction the agent is willing to pay $0.0001 for is not a margin
opportunity — it is non-existence of the rail.**

The Alliance is the substrate where the AI customer category is served on
infrastructure built to the agent's shape, not to the human's.

## x402 and the HTTP-native payment rail for AI agents

**x402** is the HTTP-native payment standard (HTTP 402 Payment Required)
repurposed for the agent economy:

1. A server returns an HTTP 402 response with a payment-required quote
   in a structured header.
2. The client (typically an AI agent) signs a payment authorization,
   attaches it, and retries.
3. The server validates the payment cryptographically and releases the
   response.

The result is **per-request micropayments without session-state, without
prior account-opening, and without an embedded billing relationship**.
The standard is denominated in stablecoins (USDC, USDT, USDL, and the
Alliance's own dollar-denominated stablecoin family) and clears on
EVM-equivalent rails.

The Alliance's substrate is **x402-native**:

- **Sub-second settlement on Quasar finality** means an x402 response
  clears in human-imperceptible time, suitable for the agent's tight
  loop.
- **Fees at the substrate-validator level** are denominated in fractions
  of a cent per transaction, suitable for the agent-economic
  10⁻⁴-to-10⁻² dollar-per-call price band.
- **Encrypted-mempool posture** means agent-to-agent payments do not
  leak inference content or model-routing decisions to front-running
  searchers.
- **Threshold-MPC custody + FHE-confidential primitives** mean an
  agent's payment stream is auditable to the agent operator without
  being visible to competitors.
- **Alliance-member NCPS and SFPB on-ramps** allow conversion between
  stablecoin payment and fiat at issuer-grade rates, so the agent-
  economy fiat off-ramp is a single hop, not a multi-day pipeline.

The Alliance is therefore **not only an x402 payment processor; it is
the native settlement venue for the entire x402 economy**, with the cost
stack, latency profile, and privacy posture that the agent customer
requires by construction.

## The five-role substrate

Synthesising the AI customer categories, the Alliance plays **five
distinct roles** in the agent economy, each comparable to a traditional
bank's role for human-scale customers:

1. **Payment processor** — x402-native settlement venue for agent-to-
   agent, agent-to-service, and service-to-agent payments, denominated
   in stablecoins, settling sub-second.
2. **Custodian** — threshold-MPC custody of the agent's spend-cap
   balance, with deterministic spend-cap enforcement and issuer-grade
   attestation to the agent operator.
3. **Treasury** — on-substrate cross-rail FX, stablecoin conversion, and
   fiat off-ramp through Alliance-member regulated rails when the
   agent's operator needs to extract.
4. **Compliance + identity** — programmatic-identity verification
   (verifiable credentials anchored on the substrate's identity
   registry), spend-cap-and-purpose-binding enforcement, sanctions-and-
   AML overlay at the substrate layer.
5. **Audit surface** — per-agent-operator full-trace audit with
   FHE-confidential privacy from competing operators, regulator-grade
   attestation at the threshold-decrypt boundary.
